Pain out of proportion to physical exam:

  • Deep space infection such as necrotizing fasciitis and myonecrosis
  • Compartment syndrome
  • Vascular occlusion
    • mesenteric ischemia
    • ovarian torsion
    • solid organ infarct
  • Back pain from spinal cord compression due to disk herniation, tumor, abscess or hematoma
  • Aortic dissection/aneurysm
  • Occult fractures
  • Referred pain from various sources
  • Reflex sympathetic dystrophy
  • Intractable headaches
