• Eosinophilia
    • Allergic
      • Drug side effect
        •      Penicillins
        • Tetracyclines
          •      Minocycline (especially)
        •      Clarithromycin
        •      Tetrazepam
        •      Mefloquine
      • Toxins
        • Eosinophilia-myalgia syndrome
          •      Cashew nuts
        •      Toxic oil syndrome
    • Infectious
      • Parasitic infection
        • Helminths
          •      Toxocariasis (especially)
          •      Trichinellosis
          •      Strongyloidosis
          •      Taeniasis (rarely)
          •      Cysticercosis (rarely)
    • Malignancy
      •      Hodgkin's disease, other lymphomas
      •      Myeloproliferative diseases
      •      Systemic mastocytosis
    • Autoimmune diseases
      •      Eosinophilic granulomatosis with polyangiitis
      •      Sarcoidosis
      •      Churg-Strauss syndrome
      •      Pemphigus vulgaris
      •      Eosinophilic gastrointestinal diseases
      •      Inflammatory bowel disease
    •      Hypereosinophilic syndrome
