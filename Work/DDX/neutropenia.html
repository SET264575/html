• Neutropenia
    • Categories:
      •      Mild: 1000 to 1500/mcL
      •      Moderate: 500 to 1000/mcL
      •      Severe: <500/mcL - endogenous microbial flora can cause infection
      •      Versy severe: <200/mcL - inflammatory response is muted
    • Acute neutropenia
      • Rapid neutrophil use or destruction
        •      Infection
    • Impaired production
      •      Hypoplastic bone marrow
      •      Radiation exposure
      •      Tumor infiltration of bone marrow
      •      Myelofibrosis
    •      Excessive splenic sequestration
    • Drug
      •      quinidine
      •      aminopyrine
      •      cephalosporin
      •      sulfonamides
      •      hydralazine
      •      penicillins
      •      heavy metals
      •      phenothiazine
    • Autoimmune
      •      Rheumatoid arthritis (Felty syndrome)
      •      Inflammatory bowel disease
      •      Chronic immune hepatitis
      •      Granulomatosis with polyangiitis
      •      Hodgkin lymphoma
      •      Sjogren syndrome
