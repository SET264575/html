Entering Consults in Meditech
If you call a consulting service to see a patient in the hospital (for example, calling ortho when you admit a patient to the hospitalist), please Enter a Consult Order in Meditech. The consultant gets that patient added to their list. If you don't do this, the consult may fall through the cracks.

Pharmacology
Phenergan
Some providers have ordered phenergan IV. That option isn't available in Meditech for a reason. The complications from extravasation are substantial, and it is hospital policy that it NOT be given IV. Please do not insist. Give it IM or try something else equally as effective (Metoclopramide, Prochlorperazine, Haloperidol, Droperidol).

Radiology
Contrast Allergy
The radiology techs have been instructed to be strict about their contrast allergy protocol. Please don't insist that they change their process If you want a patient to get different medications for contrast allergy, or you don't want to wait the one hour required by their protocol, discuss with the radiologist on call. Leave the tech alone.

Sedation in IR
Sedated patients being transferred from the ED to IR must be on Versed and Fentanyl. They cannot be on Propofol.
