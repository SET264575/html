Do/Order these things before you call a surgeon:

  • INR if on Coumadin
  • Consider ordering antibiotics (or be prepared to ask the surgeon what antibiotics they prefer and when)
  • NG tube for SBO
  • IV fluids if needed
  • Have ER attending see patient

Things to know before talking to surgeons:
  • NPO status
  • Previous revelant surgeries (any abdominal surgeries)
  • Weight (elevated BMI causes problems)
  • Blood thinners (if on Coumadin, what their INR is)
  • Complicating factors (other health problems) that imply patient should be admitted to hospitalist
  • CT report
Also, if this is a post-op problem:
  • Date of previous procedure
  • Name of surgeon
  • Type of procedure
  • Were there any known complications at the time of procedure
Specifically for Neurosurgeons:
  • Blood thinners
  • GCS/Neuro exam
  • Blood pressure
  • PT/PTT, type & screen have been ordered
  • If head bleed and systolic blood pressure > 140, IV antihypertensives given
