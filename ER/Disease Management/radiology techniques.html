Checking if internal/external transhepatic biliary drain is patent:

  1. Ask Ct tech for 5 cc of Gastrograffin (they will probably send a 30 cc bottle)
  2. Flush the drain with 10 cc saline
  3. Flush the drain with 10 cc of 50% Gastrograffin and 50% saline
  5. Flush the drain with 10 cc saline
  6. Order a noncontrast CT to look for contrast in the small intestine
