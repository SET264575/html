Our Locations

Berrywood Clinic

3401 Berrywood Dr.
Columbia, Missouri 65201

Get Directions 573-777-8300

Hours:
Monday-Friday, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.

Services offered at this location:

Therapy & Counseling

Psychological Evaluations

Psychiatric

Child and Adolescent Specialty Services/Case Management

Pharmacy

Administration

Walk-In Clinic

In addition to serving as the primary administrative office for Burrell's Central Region, the Berrywood facility also houses a Walk-in Clinic (Monday-Friday, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.) and our outpatient clinic for psychological and psychiatric services, as well as the Child and Adolescent Clinic for the Central Region. Berrywood is also home to the Burrell Pharmacy.
