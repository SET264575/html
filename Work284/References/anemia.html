Anemia

Initial Orders

  • CBC
  • CMP
  • Type & screen
  • PT/INR
  • PTT
  • Fecal occult blood

  • Consider:
    • Reticulocyte count
    • Haptoglobin
    • Ferritin
    • Iron profile
    • DIC panel
    • Vitamin B12
    • Folate
    • CT chest and/or abdomen & pelvis

Initial Treatment

  • Normal saline
  • Transfusion
  • Anticoagulation reversal

Differential Diagmosis

DDX based on RBC size
Blood loss:
  • Trauma
  • Upper GI bleeding
  • Lower GI bleeding
  • Vaginal bleeding
  • Internal bleeding
    • Retroperitoneal hemorrhage
    • Intraperitoneal hemorrhage
    • Intrathoracic hemorrhage
Decreased Production:
  • Vitamin B12 deficiency
  • Folate deficiency
  • Myelodysplastic syndrome
  • Leukemia
  • Aplastic anemia
Increased Destruction
  • Hemolytic anemia
