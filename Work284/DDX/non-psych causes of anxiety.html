• Non-psychiatric causes of anxiety symptoms
    • Drugs
      • Alcohol
        •      Alcoholism
        •      Alcohol withdrawal
      • Medication or substance abuse
        •      Caffeine
        •      Nicotine
        •      Albuterol
        •      Levothyroxine
        •      Decongestants (e.g. pseudoephedrine)
        • Medication or substance withdrawal
          •      Alcohol
          •      Benzodiazepines
          •      Opioids
          •      Antidepressants
      • Illicit drug use
        •      Cannabis use
        •      Cannabis use disorder
        •      Cocaine
        •      Amphetamines
        •      Hallucinogens
    • Endocrine
      •      Hypoglycemia
      •      Hyperthyroidism
      •      Thyrotoxicosis
      •      Hyperparathyroidism
      •      Pheochromocytoma
      •      Carcinoid

    • Heart
      •      Arrhythmia (e.g. SVTs, etc)

      •      Obstructive pulmonary diseases
      •      Asthma
      •      ARDS
      •      Pneumothorax
    •      Hypoxemia
    •      Serotonin syndrome
    •      Paraneoplastic syndrome

    • Neurologic
      •      Postconcussion syndrome

      •      Transient ischemic attacks

    •      Restless legs syndrome
    • Electrolyte imbalance
      •      Hypercalcemia
    •      Delirium
    •      Ehlers Danlos (Hypermobility syndrome)
