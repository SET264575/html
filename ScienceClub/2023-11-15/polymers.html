Core Concepts

  • Carbon is special because it commonly created bonds with itself and has 4 bonds, allows a very large number of molecules to be made with it.
  • Small molecules can be used as building blocks to make large molecules.
  • Macromolecules can contain thousands of atoms.
  • Most of the largest molecules are polymers.
  • The four major polymers in the human body are carbohydrates, proteins, DNA and fats.
  • The three major macronutrients in our diet are carbohydrates, proteins and fats.
